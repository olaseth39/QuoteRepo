Thank you for using Quotation Formulator. The whole idea is to create a seamless quotation(GRP & STEEL tanks) with no need of racking ones brain for best price dimension(s). You are implored to read through this guard to best use of the application. The whole quotation set is so sensitive that it gives the dimension(s) for the best price as in number of panels for STEEL tanks and height for GRP tanks. There are some instances where more than one dimension gives the best dimension. The set-up is sensitive and sophisticated enough to suggest the better/best as the case may be, in terms of space. However, it is up to the user to chose between the suggested dimension by the set-up and the alternative dimension as given on the top of the quote where it applicable. Not in all cases will you have exactly the volume you have entered. The set-up will give the closest value higher than the required volume where the required volume is not feasible to come by. You can reduce the input volume if you want something lower than the volume you want.

  1. Note that the required volume MUST be in liters
  2. For STEEL tank, unit length is 1.22m, while for GRP tank, unit length is 1m

  4. The first quote is for engineers looking to get a quick quotation for their client or for best dimension to use
